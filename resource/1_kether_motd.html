Welcome to Kether Server!

Here you can find some most useful commands:

  • !r, !nr | F1, F2 (KB keys) — Sets your ready status

  • !s — Switches you to spectator's team

  • !vts [nick] — Calls a voting to switch the given player to spectators

  • !tank, !boss — Shows current tank and witch spawn points (in percentages)

  • !current — Shows current flow (in percentages)

  • !chmatch — Calls a voting to change the game config

  • !mix — Calls a voting to mix teams

  • !voteboss [tank] [witch] — Calls a voting to set certain tank and witch spawn points (in percentages)

  • !hat — (VIP) Opens a menu with wearable hats

  • !lerps

Here you can download all the custom maps installed on this server with just one click!
